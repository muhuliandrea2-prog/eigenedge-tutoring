Our Tutoring Philosophy

Beyond Memorization

At EigenEdge, we move beyond rote memorization. We specialize in transforming abstract numbers into logical, manageable concepts.

  • Creative Mnemonics: We use memorable acronyms and rhymes to help students recall complex formulas during high-pressure exams.
  • Past Paper Mastery: We focus on solving previous exam papers to build "exam stamina" and familiarize students with specific question phrasing.
  • Logical Intuition: We teach the why behind the math, ensuring students can solve unfamiliar problems independently.

Service Delivery

Modern Learning Technology

We utilize modern technology to ensure online learning is interactive and engaging.

  • Format: One-on-one online sessions via Zoom or Google Meet.
  • Real-Time Tablet Integration: Using a professional Tablet & Stylus (iPad), I write out problems by hand just like a real teacher on a chalkboard. Students see every step appear instantly on their screen.
  • Revision Resources: After every lesson, a PDF summary of the handwritten notes is generated and sent to the student for their revision records.

Frequency & Scheduling

Flexible Timetables

We offer flexible schedules tailored to the student's current needs:

  • Exam Intensive (2+ Sessions/Week): Recommended during test weeks or for students needing to catch up quickly on missed concepts.
